Staking rules

PCF will be distributed via farming: no pre-mine, no founder shares, no VC interests - simply equal-opportunity staking distribution to attract a broad and vision-aligned community to steward the future of the protocol and token.

How to mine PCF

You will need Metamask to mine PCF. Choose the pool and click on Approve. Wait for a few seconds and you will see stake options.
